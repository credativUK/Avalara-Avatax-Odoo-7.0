Sales tax is hard. We make it easy.

The Avatax calc app by Kranbery automates the complex task of sales tax calculation with ease. Sale tax calculations are based on automatically validated shop, warehouse and customer address. This app plugs into your current installation of odoo with minimal configuration and just works. Your common transactions are calculated and stored by Avalara's calc service returning the proper sales tax and places the tax into the order/invoice in real-time seamlessly.
